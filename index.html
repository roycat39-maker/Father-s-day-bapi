Today, I want you to feel what I feel every day: immense, unshakeable pride in being your son. Thank you for being my Bapi.

Your quiet strength

You never raised your voice to prove a point. I learned what real strength looks like — not in force, but in calm, in patience, in showing up without complaint.

Days by the sea

Those beach trips were more than holidays. You'd put your earphones in, let the sea breeze take over, and for a moment I'd see you completely free. I treasure that image.

Dressed our finest

Standing beside you at celebrations, I felt something I couldn't name then but can now — dignity. You walk into any room and everyone notices. I'm so proud to be beside you.

Ordinary evenings

The warmth of our home, your presence in the next room — those unremarkable evenings that I now realise were the most remarkable gift of my life.
